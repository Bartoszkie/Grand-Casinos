Crown Casino Perth

Formally known as Burswood Casino Perth, the Crown Casino is the largest casino in Western Australia, boasting a large entertainment complex comprising of two luxury hotels, golf course, convention centre, as well as multiple bars and restaurants. Not surprisingly, the Crown Casino Perth is one of the go-to destinations for entertainment and nightlife.

The casino itself offers a variety of games and machines spread across 1500 sq ft, and is open 24/7. But this is just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to entertainment. With 16 restaurants, fine dining is high on the agenda for guests at the Crown Perth, with our top pick being Modo Mio which offers authentic Italian cuisine that celebrates the heart of Italian culture, where food and family are central to everyday life

Unlike other casinos in Australia, sports are a big part of the entertainment at the Crown Perth. The 18 hole golf course is a key attraction for those looking to play a little, but there are also ample opportunities to watch sports at the 21,000 capacity Burswood Dome which has hosted a range of sports from tennis to soccer, and even WWE wrestling. The arena also acts as major venue for music acts in the city, and has an impressive list of recent performances including Taylor Swift, Lady Gaga, Kylie Minogue, and Guns N Roses!
